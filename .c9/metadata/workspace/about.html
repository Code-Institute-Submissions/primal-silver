What Do I Do?

\n

I create individual hand made peices of jewellery, drawing influence from the natrual world around me. Working primarily in Hemel Hempstead, i started to use my love of nature and the outdoors to design and create stunning pieces of silver\n jewellerey. It excites me to know there is such love for the work i create, and i look forward to making a bespoke piece for you!

\n
\n

How & Why?

\n

I use number of methods & techniques to craft my work, including wax carving and casting.

I love the freedom of creating pieces which reflect my own interests & passions, the appreciation and gratitude i get from everyone i make\n something for! It makes it all worthwhile.
